> [!NOTE] Tips for committing
> - Use an active voice: "Fix card generator"
> - Avoid using vague commit messages such as "save" or "updated"
> - Commit early and often!
> - Github has a 72-character limit so we recommend keeping your commit's subject to within this amount;
